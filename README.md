# Abstract
This project presents an AI-Driven Emotion-Aware Video Editing Tool that automatically enhances videos based on detected human emotions. The system analyzes facial expressions and audio from the input video to identify emotions such as happiness, sadness, or anger. Based on the detected emotion, the tool applies suitable edits like color adjustments, background music, and transitions to improve the overall impact of the video. By combining Artificial Intelligence, computer vision, and video processing techniques, this system reduces manual editing effort and helps create more engaging and emotionally expressive videos efficiently.
# Introduction
In today’s digital era, video content plays an important role in communication and storytelling. However, manual video editing to match emotional tone is time-consuming and requires skill. This project introduces an AI-Driven Emotion-Aware Video Editing Tool that automatically detects emotions from video content and applies suitable editing effects like color changes, music, and transitions. This makes video editing faster, easier, and more effective.

## Objectives:
* To develop an AI-based system for detecting emotions from video.
* To automate video editing based on detected emotions.
* To enhance video quality using effects like filters, music, and transitions.
* To reduce manual effort and time in video editing.
* To improve user experience and storytelling through AI.
# Literature Review
Recent research in Artificial Intelligence and computer vision has enabled systems to detect human emotions from facial expressions and voice. Tools like DeepFace and OpenCV are widely used for emotion detection and video processing. Studies show that AI-based editing techniques can enhance video quality by applying automated effects. However, most existing systems focus only on emotion detection or manual editing, and lack full automation. This project combines emotion recognition with automatic video editing to create a more efficient and intelligent solution.
